About the Event

In 1931,the Lesotho Football Association(LeFA) was established.
The national team(Likuena) has participated in various
international competitions,including the Council Of Southern Africa
Football Association(COSAFA)Cup and FIFA World Cup qualifiers

Importance

  • Economic growth:

    • Attracting tournaments and fans can boost local economy through tourism and job creation

  • Youth development:

    • Encouraging young talent can lead to careers

  • National pride:

    • Success in soccer can unite the nation and showcase Lesotho globally

    Target tourists

  1. Soccer enthusiasts(fans)
  2. Sports tourists
  3. Families and youth
  4. Local business owners
  5. Media and photographers
